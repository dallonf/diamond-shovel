Diamond Shovel

Diamond Shovel is a way to find and schedule competitive Minecraft games. It's perfect if you want to find some people to play a round of The Hunger Games, The Walls, or even Redstone Team Fortress 2!

Diamond Shovel is not a server browser - it's not meant to find a long-running Survival server or a game starting right now. You can sign up for a game that starts hours, even days, in the future. That way, there's plenty of time to find players. There's nothing worse than joining a game on your own!

To start using Diamond Shovel, just register and sign up for an upcoming game. You can also create your own game - just make sure your computer can handle it; hosts are expected to provide their own servers.

Have fun, and good luck on the blocky battlefield! If you have any questions, bug reports, or feature requests, send an email to dallon@deployd.com.

Diamond Shovel is built on Deployd, a platform that makes web apps like this one simple and fun to create.
